Resources for Current Students

So you’ve registered for orientation, emailed with your academic advisor, packed for your big move to the city, and are ready to settle into life as an Illinois Tech Scarlet Hawk. What’s next?

Explore the city with your U-Pass. Find your favorite place to study. Try all the food. Talk to your favorite professor about their research. Conduct your own research. Create your own club. Discover your own path at Illinois Tech.

If you have a question or need someone to talk to, Illinois Tech faculty, staff, and student leaders are here to help. Don’t hesitate to reach out to your academic adviser, an academic coach or ELM certified peer mentor, resident adviser or first-year mentor, or the dean of students with your questions or concerns.
